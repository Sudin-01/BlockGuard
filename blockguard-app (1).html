How policy works per agent
agent_a — Baseline
No policy engine. All actions permitted regardless of risk. No logs. Nothing written to chain.
agent_b — Centralized
Policy stored as Python dict in policy_engine.py. Logs to SQLite. Resets on Flask restart. No blockchain.
agent_c — Blockchain
Policy in smart contract on Ganache. Every decision calls checkPermission(). Changes need setPermission() tx. All decisions logged on-chain via logDecision().
